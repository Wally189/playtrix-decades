Privacy

Short version
PlayTriX is designed to respect your privacy. There are no accounts, no hidden tracking scripts and no data being sent to a server from this page.

What PlayTriX does

On your device only
PlayTriX uses your browser’s built-in storage (for example localStorage) to remember:
  • which theme you’ve chosen,
  • which tiles and favourites you’ve set up,
  • whether you’ve turned on the password lock (once that’s wired in),
  • and other settings that make the console feel like yours.
This information never leaves your device unless you choose to export a backup file yourself.

What PlayTriX does not do

No tracking, no ads, no analytics
PlayTriX does not:
  • create online accounts for you,
  • track your browsing on other sites,
  • show adverts or sell your data,
  • send your tiles or notes to a central server.
It’s a static set of pages designed to sit quietly on your device as a personal console.

Backups & your responsibility

You stay in control
When you use the backup tools on the Settings page, you create a file or a block of text that represents your PlayTriX setup. That backup is yours:
  • you choose where to store it,
  • you decide who can see it,
  • you can delete it at any time.
If you share a backup with someone else, they will be able to load a copy of your console on their own device – this can be intentional (for example, giving someone a starter layout).

Clearing data

How to wipe PlayTriX
Because PlayTriX lives inside your browser, clearing it is as simple as clearing that browser’s stored data for this site. The exact steps vary by browser, but generally:
  • Open your browser’s settings.
  • Look for “Site settings”, “Storage” or similar.
  • Find the entry for the PlayTriX site and clear its data.
After that, PlayTriX will return to its first-run state. If you have a backup, you can import it to restore your console.
