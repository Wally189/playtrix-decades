Lock screen

A simple extra layer of privacy
The PlayTriX password is an optional extra lock. It does not replace the PIN, passcode or password on your phone or laptop – it simply keeps your console private if someone else opens this page.
Everything here is stored on your device only. No passwords are ever sent to a server.

Unlock PlayTriX

Enter password
When the lock screen is active, you’ll be asked for your PlayTriX password before the Console opens.
In this early version, the lock behaves as a design pattern. The full logic can be wired into the PlayTriX engine so that the Console is hidden until the correct password is entered.

Set or change password

Choose a password that you’ll remember
The aim is not bank-level security – it’s to stop casual snooping if a colleague, friend or family member opens PlayTriX on your device.
Once wired into the engine, this panel will store your password securely in local storage and turn on the lock screen automatically.

Remove password

Turn the lock screen off
If you decide you don’t need the PlayTriX lock, you’ll be able to remove it here.
Once the full logic is in place, this will clear the saved password and return PlayTriX to a no-lock state on this device.
